About CHR

Our Mission

Come-Here Rider (CHR) empowers tricycle drivers and connects communities through reliable delivery services, bringing convenience to remote locations where traditional delivery services struggle to reach.

  • Order history and status updates

Payment System
  • Cash-on-delivery payments
  • Commission-based earnings
  • Transparent pricing structure
  • Instant payment processing
Account Management

  • Secure authentication
  • Profile management tools
  • Organization oversight
